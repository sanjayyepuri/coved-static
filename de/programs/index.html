CovEd now offers Art, Book, and Chess club for K-12 students! Students can learn art skills and make creative projects, read and discuss books, learn and play chess, and make new friends from all over America!

You can view our class catalog here. Some of our most popular classes are:

  • James and the Giant Peach book club, recommended for 1st and 2nd graders
  • Percy Jackson book club, recommended for 5th through 8th graders
  • Brave New World book club, recommended for High School students
  • Abstract acrylic and oil painting, recommended for students who are artistically inclined and want to grow their skills
  • Drawing techniques, recommended for students who are artistically inclined and want to grow their skills
  • Beginner Chess Club, for students new to the game
  • Intermediate Chess Club, for students who want to improve their play via learning openings and tactics

Classes are offered for ALL ages and skill levels. Students can sign up for whichever class they want!

If you are interested in signing your student up for these clubs, go to bit.ly/coved-abc-student.
